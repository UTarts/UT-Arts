Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 9, 2025

UT Arts ("us", "we", or "our") operates the utarts.in website (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide our services and communicate with you.

Types of Data Collected:

  • Personal Data: When you use our contact form or commission a project, we collect personally identifiable information, including your Name, Email Address, and Phone Number.
  • Project Data: To provide our services, you may send us materials such as project briefs or reference photographs for portraits. This data is used solely to complete your project.
  • Usage Data: We may in the future use analytics services (like Google Analytics) to collect information on how the Service is accessed and used (e.g., IP address, browser type, pages visited).

2. How We Use Your Data

UT Arts uses the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our Service
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide quotes
  • To fulfill your project (e.g., build your website, create your portrait)
  • To process payments and send invoices
  • To provide customer support
  • To monitor and analyze usage to improve our Service

3. Project Data & Reference Photos

We take the privacy of your project materials seriously. All client-provided reference photographs for portrait commissions are considered confidential and will be securely deleted **30 days after the completion** and final delivery of your project.

4. Third-Party Data Processors

We use third-party services to operate our business. These services have their own privacy policies and are responsible for handling your data.

  • Jotform: Our contact form is powered by Jotform. When you submit our contact form, your data (name, email, phone, project details) is processed by Jotform to notify us of your inquiry.
  • Google Analytics (Future Use): We plan to use Google Analytics to understand our website traffic. This service tracks and reports website usage and may share data with other Google services.

5. Data Security

The security of your data is important to us. We use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Data, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee its absolute security.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • By email: **info@utarts.in**
